Today's Beauty Tip

Post-Treatment Sunscreen Is Essential

After any facial treatment or procedure, your skin becomes more sensitive to UV damage. Always apply a broad-spectrum SPF 50 sunscreen before leaving our clinic and continue to reapply every 2 hours when outdoors, even on cloudy days. This simple habit not only protects your skin but also extends the benefits of your treatment. Look for mineral-based formulas with zinc oxide or titanium dioxide for gentle yet effective protection.
